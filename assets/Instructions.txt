Collect resources by assigning dwellers to rooms, each SPECIAL skill will affect how many resources can be collected.

 -- NOTICE --
saving and loading may not work properly because it is being redone.

Strength - Power.
Perception - Water. 
Endurance - Defense.
Intelligence - Stimpacks.
Charisma - Happiness & breeding.
Agility - Food.
Luck - Slight bonus to all.

Rooms can be upgraded and linked together for higher rewards and cheaper upgrades.

Dwellers can be bred in the Lounge to produce children that grow overtime.
